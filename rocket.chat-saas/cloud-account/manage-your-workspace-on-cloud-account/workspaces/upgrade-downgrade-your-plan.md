# Upgrade/Downgrade your Plan

To upgrade/downgrade your plan:

1. Click **Change Plan**, as shown below:

![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (129).png>)

2\. Select your desired plan.

![](<../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (130).png>)

Your new plan is in place.
